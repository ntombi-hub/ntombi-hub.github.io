03 — Projects

⭐ Featured Project · Data Engineering · Community Impact
💧 Amanzi Soweto — Water Intelligence Platform

An end-to-end data pipeline that scrapes Johannesburg Water's daily notices, filters them for Soweto suburbs, classifies outages by severity (HIGH / MEDIUM / LOW), stores them in a PostgreSQL database, and sends residents real-time WhatsApp & SMS alerts — suburb by suburb. Built because Soweto residents deserved to know when and why their water was off.

The pipeline runs every 2 hours via Apache Airflow, covers 25+ Soweto suburbs across 4 reservoir zones, and uses Twilio for WhatsApp notifications — the primary communication channel in townships.
